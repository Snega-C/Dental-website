Clinical Specialties

Painless, Aesthetic, and Bio-Safe Solutions

We provide full-spectrum oral care under one roof. Our modern operatories are engineered for stress-free dentistry, utilising high-definition cameras and soft-tissue lasers.

General Dentistry

Keep your teeth and gums healthy with routine checkups, comprehensive diagnostics, and personalized oral health guidance.

General dentistry services include digital diagnostics, dental plaque scaling, fluoride applications, fillings, and early-stage gum treatment options.

Teeth Cleaning

Deep-cleaning scaling and polishing procedures using ultrasonic scalers to remove stains, plaque, and stubborn tartar buildup.

Our advanced ultrasonic cleaning removes sub-gingival calculus smoothly, followed by fine air-polishing to remove coffee and tea staining.

Root Canal Treatment

Painless micro-endodontic therapy to save severely damaged or infected teeth, eliminating pain and restoring chew strength.

We perform root canal therapy using electronic apex locators and digital rotary files, ensuring high precision, speed, and safety.

Dental Implants

Permanently replace single or multiple missing teeth with bio-compatible implants topped with custom porcelain crowns.

Implants provide a permanent anchor. We use advanced computer-guided surgery templates for absolute structural alignment and minimal healing.

Orthodontics

Realign misaligned teeth and incorrect bites using standard low-profile braces or customized, removable clear aligners.

We offer Invisalign clear alignment templates and ceramic braces. Our orthodontic team maps out digital movements for faster, predictable results.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your visual appearance. Perfect chip cracks, gaps, shapes, and contours using premium veneers and dental bonding.

Veneers are ultra-thin shells of porcelain bonded to the front teeth to create beautiful, symmetrical smiles.

Teeth Whitening

Brighten your teeth up to 8 shades in a single appointment using advanced medical whitening lasers with zero enamel damage.

We apply a protective barrier to your gums, coat teeth in hydrogen peroxide gel, and activate it with a specialized Zoom laser.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, patient-focused dental care tailored for children, creating positive clinic experiences from a young age.

We offer dental sealants, decay prevention, and gentle pediatric procedures in our friendly, custom-decorated rooms.
